Complaints Procedure

Last updated: 16 March 2026

We aim to provide an excellent experience for every customer. If something has gone wrong, we want to hear about it and put it right as quickly as possible. This procedure applies to all customers — consumer and trade — for products, services, and delivery.

Our Complaints Process

  1. 1

    Contact Us Directly

    Most issues can be resolved quickly by getting in touch. Email info@drmobilephone.ie, call 045 949 611, or visit us in store. Please include your order reference number and a clear description of the issue.

  2. 2

    Formal Written Complaint

    If your issue is not resolved to your satisfaction after initial contact, you may raise a formal written complaint by emailing info@drmobilephone.ie with the subject line "Formal Complaint" and include:

    Your name and contact details  ·  Your order reference  ·  A clear description of the complaint  ·  What outcome you are seeking

  3. 3

    We Respond & Investigate

    We will acknowledge your complaint within 2 working days and aim to provide a full response and resolution within 14 working days. If we need longer we will tell you why and give you a revised timeframe.

  4. 4

    If You Are Still Unsatisfied

    If you are not happy with our response, you can escalate to an external body — see the section below. We will always cooperate fully with external dispute resolution.

Our Response Commitments

Acknowledgement Within 2 working days of receiving your complaint
Full response Within 14 working days (we will update you if this is not possible)
Refunds (where applicable) Within 14 days of confirming your entitlement
Replacements (where applicable) Dispatched as soon as stock allows after agreement

Returns & Refund Complaints

If your complaint relates to a return, refund, or a faulty product, please also read our Returns & Refunds Policy which sets out your statutory rights in detail. You have the right to a repair, replacement, or refund for faulty goods under the Consumer Rights Act 2022, and a 14-day right of cancellation for online orders.

External Escalation

If we have been unable to resolve your complaint within a reasonable time, or if you are dissatisfied with our final response, you have the right to contact the following bodies free of charge:

Competition and Consumer Protection Commission (CCPC)

The CCPC enforces consumer rights law in Ireland and provides free information and advice to consumers.

Website: www.ccpc.ie

Helpline: 01 402 5555 (Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm)

EU Online Dispute Resolution (ODR) Platform

For online purchases, EU consumers can use the European Commission's ODR platform to seek resolution without going to court. We are required by EU law to make you aware of this platform.

Website: ec.europa.eu/consumers/odr

Our ODR contact email: info@drmobilephone.ie

Small Claims Court

For disputes involving amounts up to €2,000, consumers may use the Small Claims procedure through the Irish Courts Service — a low-cost and straightforward process.

Website: www.courts.ie/small-claims

Fee: €25

Data Protection Complaints

If your complaint relates to how we have handled your personal data, please refer to our Privacy Policy. For unresolved data protection issues you may contact the Data Protection Commission:
