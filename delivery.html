Delivery Timeframes

Estimated delivery times are for orders placed before 2pm on working days (Monday to Friday, excluding Irish public holidays). Orders placed after 2pm or on a weekend/public holiday will be processed the next working day.

Standard Delivery: 3–7 working days within the Republic of Ireland

Express Delivery: 1–3 working days (where available)

Delivery timeframes are estimates and not guaranteed. Delays can occasionally occur due to carrier issues, high demand periods, or circumstances outside our control.

Where We Deliver

We currently deliver to all addresses within the Republic of Ireland. We do not currently offer standard delivery to Northern Ireland or Great Britain. If you require delivery outside the Republic of Ireland, please contact us at info@drmobilephone.ie to discuss options.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched you will receive a dispatch notification by email. You can also view the status of your order at any time from your account orders page.

If you have not received your order within the estimated timeframe, please contact us and we will investigate with the carrier.

Delivery Attempt & Missed Deliveries

If a delivery attempt is unsuccessful, the carrier will typically leave a card or send a notification with instructions for re-delivery or collection from a local depot. If you have difficulty collecting your parcel, contact us and we will assist.

Damaged or Missing Items

If your order arrives damaged or items are missing, please:

  • Take photographs of the packaging and contents
  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery at info@drmobilephone.ie or call 045 949 611
  • Retain all original packaging until we have resolved the issue

We will arrange a replacement or refund for any items confirmed as damaged in transit or missing from your order. Under the Consumer Rights Act 2022, risk of damage remains with us until the goods are delivered to you.

Failed Delivery & Returned Parcels

If a parcel is returned to us because it could not be delivered (e.g. incorrect address provided, uncollected from depot), we will contact you to arrange re-delivery. A re-delivery charge may apply to cover carrier costs. If you do not respond within 14 days of our notification, we will process a refund for the goods value minus the original shipping cost.

Your Right to Cancel

For all online orders, you have the right to cancel within 14 days of receiving your goods under the EU Consumer Rights Directive. This is in addition to your rights for faulty or misdescribed goods. See our Returns & Refunds Policy for full details and how to initiate a return.

Wholesale & Trade Orders

Delivery for wholesale and trade account orders is handled separately and agreed at account opening. Please contact us directly to discuss bulk delivery requirements.
