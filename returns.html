1. Your Right to Cancel (14-Day Cooling-Off Period)

If you ordered online or by distance, you have a statutory right to cancel your order within 14 calendar days of receiving your goods — no reason needed. This right is provided by the European Union (Consumer Information, Cancellation and Other Rights) Regulations 2013 (S.I. No. 484 of 2013).

Exceptions — When the Cooling-Off Right Does Not Apply

The 14-day right of cancellation does not apply to:

  • Goods that have been unsealed after delivery and cannot be returned for health protection or hygiene reasons (e.g. in-ear headphones or earbuds where the hygiene seal has been broken)
  • Goods made to your specifications or clearly personalised
  • Services that have been fully performed with your prior consent (e.g. a completed phone repair)
  • In-store purchases — the cooling-off period applies to distance and off-premises contracts only

2. Faulty, Damaged, or Not As Described Goods

Under the Consumer Rights Act 2022, all goods we sell must be of satisfactory quality, fit for purpose, and match their description. If your item is faulty, damaged on arrival, or significantly different from how it was described, you have the following rights:

TimeframeYour Rights
Within 30 days of delivery You may reject the goods and receive a full refund (the "short-term right to reject"). You may also choose a repair or replacement instead.
30 days to 6 months after delivery You are entitled to a repair or replacement at no cost to you. If the repair or replacement is impossible, disproportionate, or unsuccessful after one attempt, you are entitled to a full or partial refund.
6 months to 6 years after delivery You may still have rights, but the burden of proof shifts — you may be required to demonstrate that the fault existed at the time of delivery.

For faulty goods, we will cover all return postage costs. Please do not return faulty goods before contacting us — we will arrange collection or provide a prepaid label where applicable.

3. How to Return an Item — Step by Step

  1. Contact us before returning
    Email info@drmobilephone.ie with your order reference, a description of the issue, and (for faulty goods) a photo if possible. We will confirm your return is authorised and provide instructions.
  2. Pack the item securely
    Use the original packaging where possible. Include a note with your name, order reference, and reason for return. We are not responsible for items damaged in transit due to inadequate packaging.
  3. Send the item back
    For faulty goods, we will arrange postage. For cooling-off returns (change of mind), you are responsible for return postage. We recommend using a tracked service and retaining proof of postage.
  4. We inspect and process
    Once we receive the item, we will inspect it and confirm the outcome within 5 working days. We will notify you by email.
  5. Receive your refund or replacement
    Refunds are issued to your original payment method within 14 days of us receiving the goods. Replacements are dispatched as soon as stock allows.

5. Items Not Eligible for Return

The following items cannot be returned unless they are faulty or not as described:

  • In-ear headphones, earbuds, or any audio accessories where the hygiene seal has been broken after delivery
  • Items that have been personalised or custom-configured to your specification
  • Gift cards or store credit vouchers
  • Items damaged by misuse, accidental damage, or liquid after delivery
  • Software, digital products, or items with a digital component that have been downloaded or activated

6. Repair Service Warranty Returns

All repair services carried out by DrMobilePhone carry a 90-day warranty on the specific repair performed.

  • If the same fault recurs within 90 days of the repair, we will re-examine the device and re-perform the repair or issue a refund of the repair charge at no additional cost.
  • The warranty does not cover new faults, physical damage, liquid damage, or faults unrelated to the original repair.
  • To make a warranty claim, bring the device to our premises or contact us at info@drmobilephone.ie with your repair ticket number.
  • We are not liable for data loss during any repair. Always back up your device before submitting it for repair.

7. Wholesale / Trade Customer Returns

Trade account returns are governed by the terms agreed at account opening. The 14-day consumer cooling-off period and Consumer Rights Act 2022 protections do not apply to business-to-business transactions.

Goods that are genuinely faulty or were incorrectly supplied will be replaced or credited. Please contact us within 5 working days of delivery to report any discrepancies or defects.

8. In-Store Purchases

For items purchased in-store:

  • The 14-day cooling-off right does not apply to in-store purchases (it applies only to distance and off-premises contracts).
  • Your statutory rights under the Consumer Rights Act 2022 still apply in full — faulty or misdescribed goods will be refunded, repaired, or replaced.
  • Please retain your receipt. Proof of purchase is required for all in-store return requests.
